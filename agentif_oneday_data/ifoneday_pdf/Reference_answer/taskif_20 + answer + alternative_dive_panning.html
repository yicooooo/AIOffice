ssessment of Proposed Dive

Status: Unsafe

  • NDL Exceeded: At 30 meters, the no-decompression limit is 20 minutes. The proposed dive of 24 minutes exceeds this limit.
  • Safety Stop Missing: Omitting a safety stop at this depth increases the risk of decompression sickness (DCS).
  • Ascent Rate Not Specified: No ascent speed is provided. A fast ascent could lead to pulmonary barotrauma.

Recommended Alternative Dive Plan

Parameter Recommended Value Rationale
Maximum Depth 24 meters Provides more bottom time within NDL (25 min at 24m)
Bottom Time 20 minutes 5 minutes below the NDL for a safety buffer
Safety Stop 5 meters for 3 minutes Recommended for dives below 18m
Ascent Rate Max 9 meters per minute Reduces risk of barotrauma and DCS
Surface Interval At least 1 hour Allows safe off-gassing before a second dive

Summary

  • This alternative dive plan stays within the no-decompression limits.
  • Includes a mandatory safety stop and safe ascent rate.
  • Supports multi-dive planning with a proper surface interval.

All recommendations are based on the Open Water Diver Manual, Chapter 3: Dive Planning and Tables.
